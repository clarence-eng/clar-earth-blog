Sambar deer are a mostly herbivorous species of large deer found in Southeast Asia, the Indian subcontinent, and southern China. They communicate with each other via vocalizations, body language, and scent marking. Predators of sambar deer include tigers, leopards, and dholes. Although in the wild sambar deer seldom live beyond 12 years of age, they can live up to 20 to 25 years. They are listed by the IUCN (International Union for Conservation of Nature) as a vulnerable species due to poaching and industrial exploitation of habitat.

I'm a sambar deer, they come from far and near,
Tryna make me fade, make me disappear.
I'm a sambar deer, I need my forest here,
Let the trees stay standing, year after year.

I don't eat meat, still strong on my feet,
Moving through the shade in thick jungle heat.
Chainsaws buzzing, no space to roam,
Every fallen tree feels like losing my home.

Don't put me in cages, I'm not a petting zoo,
Let me live my life — me be me, you be you.
Same earth beneath us, same skies we share,
Different paths we walk, with respect and care.

I'm a sambar deer, they come from far and near,
Tryna make me fade, make me disappear.
I'm a sambar deer, I need my forest here,
Let the trees stay standing, year after year.

I'm part of the system: roots, rivers, trees,
Birds in the canopy, songs in the breeze.
Listen for a second, let the message glisten,
Nature's always speaking, but we don't always listen.

We're all connected, every life, every tree,
What happens to the forest, happens to me.
Help us all thrive, keep the balance strong,
Make the forest healthy, you know I can't be wrong.

Tigers on the prowl, moving silent, moving slow,
Guardians of the forest, everywhere they go.
Save the roar above, save the hoof below,
Save every life that makes it all grow.

I'm a sambar deer, they come from far and near,
But more and more friends keep showing up here.
Side by side, with a love unending,
With hearts and hands, the forests we're tending.

Our passion is never, never bending,
Indigenous wisdom shapes the path we're heading.
If you've got help that you can be lending,
Join us — 'cause a greener world is trending.

I'm a sambar deer, this forest is my home,
Let's keep it alive so we all can roam.
