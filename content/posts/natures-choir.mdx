Ants drum the earth
and bees strum the sky.
From the branches, a melody
the chorus of birds.

Dragonflies dance
like violins in flight.
Snails trace silver notes
that glimmer in the light.

Leaves rustle gently,
whispers in the wind.
Let us listen, let us sing
together in harmony.
