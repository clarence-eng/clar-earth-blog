---
title: Out of Time
type: poem
published: true
excerpt: "Time felt endless when we always had to pretend — merry-go-rounds would never ever have to end"
---

Time felt endless when we always had to pretend
merry-go-rounds would never ever have to end
We flew through Land of Oz in spiralling wind
mum read gentle dreams we once believed in
She kissed our heads and softened the light
but now we solve for stress each night
Fighting deadlines all alone
caught in a fierce cyclone
Lost in endeavour
feeling forever
Out of time
