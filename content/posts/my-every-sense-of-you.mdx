Missing you is like
holding a pregnant breath
sweet honey on my tongue
lips heavy and full
drinking you
in languid sips

Missing you is like
playing a guitar
folded in my arms
tucked against my chest
pressed tight to my ribs
plucking heartstrings

Missing you is like
gazing into a beautiful lake
catching your reflection
glistening
beneath the moon and stars
smiling back at me

Missing you is like
roses in our backyard
a whiff of perfume
lingering traces of you
delicate and light
musk in my bedsheets

Missing you is like
birds chirping
a soft melody
leaves rustling in the breeze
tears falling with the rain
your voice calling mine

[italic]
*You echo in my every sense*
