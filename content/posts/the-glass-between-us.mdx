Cold steel bars bruise from skin to bone
The air thickens where sorrow's sown
Hooves strike the floor, chains drag below
Blood fills the trough in crimson flow

Our gazes meet through wire and rain
A flicker burns beneath the pain
If mercy breathes beyond the gate
Set down the knife, unbolt the cage

[right]
We lock the doors and drown the cries
We count their deaths and close our eyes
We tag and weigh, then turn away
We eat, forget, call it a day

[right]
The glass between us starts to break
A tremor wakes for conscience's sake
We see their worth and so we start
To let them live inside our hearts
